Assembly Project with Technical Drawings

Software: SolidWorks

Developed a fully parametric multi-part glue stick assembly in SolidWorks, including body, cap, shaft, and seat components. Produced production-standard technical drawings in accordance with AS1100, incorporating sectional views, detail views, tolerancing, surface finish specifications, and material selection (PP copolymer and homopolymer). Key features include circular patterning on PCD, M6 threading, mass property evaluation, and complete Bill of Materials (BOM) generation. The project demonstrates proficiency in parametric modelling, assembly management, manufacturability considerations, and engineering drawing standards.
